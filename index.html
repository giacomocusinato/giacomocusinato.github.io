Hi, I'm Giacomo

I'm a Computer Science graduate with a strong passion for software development, web and technologies. In February 2017 I got my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at the University of Padua where I started to get in touch with the art of software engineering.

I started writing my first lines of code in 2012 when I was developing application for the earlier versions of Windows Phone operating system. At that time I started to get deep into the internal design and patterns of a mobile application and the principles that are now used in modern web software.

In 2015 I switched to a UNIX based development environment with which I build multiple university projects and start focusing on web.

In 2016 I attend an internship in Amsterdam in order to complete my degree. During the internship, I developed my front-end engeneering skills and I got in touch with the growing start-up ecosystem of the city.

When I'm not in front of the screen you can easily find me around the world, listening to some good music or enjoying some cheap italian wine.
