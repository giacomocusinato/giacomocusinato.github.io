Hi, I'm Giacomo

I'm a Computer Science graduate with a strong passion for software development, web and technologies. In February 2017 I got my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at the University of Padua where I started to get in touch with the art of software engineering.

I started writing my first lines of code in 2012 when I was developing application for the earlier versions of Windows Phone operating system. When I'm not in front of the screen you can easily find me around the world, listening to some good music or enjoying some cheap italian wine.
